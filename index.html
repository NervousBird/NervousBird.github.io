The Story of Te Houtaewa
Te Houtaewa was the fastest runner of his day and playedmany pranks on his peoples enemies. One morning his mother wanted kumara for the hangi(earth oven) and asked Te Houtaewa to go to the gardens at Te Kao, a short distanceaway. He agreed to fetch the kumara and told his mother to prepare the hangi.
